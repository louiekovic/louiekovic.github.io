The Melted Circus - Let The Symphony Drown

The Melted Circus was developed over the course of three years by two people attempting to make a conceptual Hip-Hop album without the use of samples. Between 2011 and 2014, Russ Taylor and Louie Kovic recorded everything within their respective home studios in Los Angeles, California. What began as a simple concept, eventually evolved into an album that exceeded their original expectations. Let The Symphony Drown is an introspective journey through one's own interpretation of life.

The YouTube playlist works well for immediacy, but we would prefer you listen to the album with gapless playback. Download the mp3s via Google Drive, Dropbox, or MEGA.
